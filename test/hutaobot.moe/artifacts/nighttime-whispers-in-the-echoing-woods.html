Available in 4★ and 5★

Bonuses:

ATK +18%.
4-Piece Set Bonus
After using an Elemental Skill, gain a 20% Geo DMG Bonus for 10s. While under a shield granted by the Crystallize reaction, the above effect will be increased by 150%, and this additional increase disappears 1s after that shield is lost.

Pieces:

Selfless Floral Accessory

A brooch that the witch in the tale wore. As with the other ornaments she loved, this was characterized by butterfly-like shapes.

Honest Quill

The witch in the tale used this dip pen. Among its various advantages, its silky-smooth writing is the least worthy of mention.

Faithful Hourglass

A sand timepiece that the witch relied on in the story. Legend has it that if you recite the wrong incantation over it, time will suddenly flow exceptionally quickly.

Magnanimous Ink Bottle

An ink bottle utilized by the witch in the story. It possesses a strange enchantment no less powerful than the one cast upon the dip pen.

Compassionate Ladies' Hat

The lady's hat favored by the witch in the story. She loved this piece of headwear for how it weaves together solemnity and playfulness.
