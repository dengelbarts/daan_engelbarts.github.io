Thundering Fury

Thundering Fury

Available in 4★ and 5★

Bonuses:

2-Piece Set Bonus
Electro DMG Bonus +15%
4-Piece Set Bonus
Increases DMG caused by Overloaded, Electro-Charged, Superconduct, and Hyperbloom by 40%, and the DMG Bonus conferred by Aggravate is increased by 20%. When Quicken or the aforementioned Elemental Reactions are triggered, Elemental Skill CD is decreased by 1s. Can only occur once every 0.8s.

Pieces:

Thunderbird's Mercy

A lightning-infused flower, somehow spared the fate of being trodden underfoot or reduced to ash by the furious purple fire, making it the sole survivor on the day of disaster.

Survivor of Catastrophe

A lightning-charged feather that still flickers with the wrath of the Thunderbird's cruel retribution.

Hourglass of Thunder

The hourglass used to foretell the coming of the Thunderbird by the tribe that worshiped it. It has fallen into eternal silence now that the tribe is no more.

Omen of Thunderstorm

A ceremonial cup that holds the blood of the innocent. It is brimming with the thundering fury of the prayers that echo within.

Thunder Summoner's Crown

A crown once worn by an ancient shaman who worshiped the Thunderbird. The capricious beast remained unmoved by the shaman's devotion.
