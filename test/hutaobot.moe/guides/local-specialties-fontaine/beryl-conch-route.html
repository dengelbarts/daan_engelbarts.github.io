Beryl Conch route

Romartime Flower route →

Beryl Conches can found in the wilderness of Fontaine, near Salacia Plain and are being sold by Hinterman (up to 5 each per 3 days).

A conch-like structure that gives off a faint glow. Despite the name, it is not a shell but something condensed from pure elemental energy.
